    FAQs

    Which plan is right for me?

    AdsPower offers 2 free profiles for starters. We also have a 7-day free trial for you to explore. If you need more profiles after the trial, feel free to customize your plan based on your requirements. Our support team is always available to assist.

    What does 'number of users' mean?

    AdsPower supports management of multiple users. To establish your AdsPower team, use a super administrator account to create accounts for your team members and assign their permissions. For additional information, refer to the "Members" tutorial in our Help Center.

    What does 'number of profiles' mean?

    In AdsPower, a profile is a unique set of browser settings, mimicking individual browser fingerprints such as User Agent, IP address, Canvas fingerprint, etc. It allows you to keep privacy across various tasks and accounts using multiple independent browser environments. Profiles can be created individually or in bulk. For more information, see the "Profile" tutorial in our Help Center.

    How can I get more profiles?

    AdsPower provides 2 free profiles for a start. If you need more, feel free to upgrade your plan via the Cost Center whenever you want.
